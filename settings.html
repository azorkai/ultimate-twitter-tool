Twitter Stats Analyzer

Version 1.2.0

A powerful extension that helps you analyze Twitter profiles, track verified followers, bulk follow users, auto interact with tweets, and block ads.

  • Track followers and unfollowers
  • Identify verified accounts following you
  • Bulk follow/unfollow users
  • Auto-interact with tweets
  • Advanced statistics and analytics
  • Ad blocking for a cleaner Twitter experience
